JetHack

A game created for Global Game Jame 2018.

5 Person Team. Lead Engineer and Level Design

As the lead engineer for our 2018 Global Game Jam team, I was in charge of creating the hacking mini-game that was the central to the game. I had a great time making it and learned a lot of neat tricks during the process, like state changes based on global objectives.

I also was tasked with fine tuning the level design and designing the world the player sees around them, making the world feel dynamic and engaging.
