Who is Christopher Cornford?

This is my story, and a little of who I am.

I grew up in Providence, Rhode Island before coming to Fitchburg State University to study Game Design. I currently live in the Greater Boston area. Since I was a child, I always wanted to make a difference in the world; even if it was just to make one person's day better. I have also loved video games my entire life; from playing on my Windows 98 desktop or my Nintendo 64, to now making games. I chose this industry because I personally have had my life changed by playing games, and hope that I can do the same for others.

I believe, that as individuals, we never truly stop learning. I don't pride myself on having an encylopedic knowledge, rather that I know how to find the information required to solve the task at hand. I never find myself afraid to ask questions, and never make others feel insecure about asking them either.

My greatest strengths include the ability to work well with other people, even under stressful circumstances. I am well-organized, and reliable. I truly commit myself to a project, and seek to better it in anyway I can, whether it be programming a new game mechanic, or slinging coffees at Dunkin' Donuts. I feel I am able to fit in with any group, no matter how diverse.
